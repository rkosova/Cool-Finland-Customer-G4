About Us

Cool-Finland Oy was founded in 2005. It is a limited company that is located in Forssa, it's primary industry is waste disposal. The CEO of the company is Pasi Tapani Kaskinen.

Cool-Finland Oy's revenue was 2,6 million and financial period was 401 thousand in 2021. The revenue increased 18%. Operating profit margin was 19,2%.

12/2021 päättyneellä tilikaudella yhtiöllä oli 13 työntekijää. Työntekijämäärä kasvoi 8,3% edelliseltä tilikaudelta. The company had 13 employees at the end of financial period in 12/2021. The amount of employees increased by 8,3% at the end of the financial period.
